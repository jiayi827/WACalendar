Public Meetings

"Meeting" means meetings at which the public agency takes “action”. All meetings of the governing body of a public agency shall be open and public and all persons shall be permitted to attend, except as otherwise provided in RCW 42.30.
Quote from RCW 42.30, RCW 42.32
