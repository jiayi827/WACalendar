Public Hearing Notice - Edmonds City Council

On October 6, 2015, the Edmonds City Council will hold a public hearing regarding the following issue:



PUBLIC HEARING BEFORE THE CITY COUNCIL ON PROPOSED AMENDMENTS TO THE CITY OF EDMONDS CRITICAL AREA REGULATIONS CONTAINED IN CHAPTERS 23.40, 23.50, 23.60, 23.70, 23.80, AND 23.90 OF THE EDMONDS COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT CODE (ECDC) AND ASSOCIATED CODE AMENDMENTS RELATED TO FREQUENTLY FLOODED AREAS IN CHAPTER 19.00 ECDC AND CHAPTER 21 ECDC.


The City Council meeting will begin at 7:00 p.m. at the Council Chambers, Public Safety Complex, 250 5th Avenue North, Edmonds, Washington. At the time and place stated above, all interested parties will be heard; however, there will be a time restriction for oral comment. Written comments are welcomed prior to or at the public hearing.

If the item is continued to another date because it is not completed, or further information is needed, the date of the continuation will be announced only at the meeting. Additional information may be obtained at the Planning Department, 121 5th Avenue N, Edmonds. Telephone: (425) 771-0220.

PUBLIC HEARING NOTICE - EDMONDS CITY COUNCIL
 
On March 21, 2017, the Edmonds City Council will hold a public hearing regarding the following issue:
 
PUBLIC HEARING ON the City of Edmonds Shoreline Master Program (SMP) Update.The City is required by the Shoreline Management Act (SMA) (RCW 90.58) to update its SMP in order to ensure compliance with the SMA and state guidelines for local SMPs.  The Department of Ecology has conditionally approved the city’s SMP, but has offerred alternative options for development standards within the Urban Mixed Use IV Shoreline Environment adjacent to the Edmonds Marsh.  The city is seeking public testimony on Ecology’s options and other potential options regarding development standards for the Urban Mixed Use IV shoreline environment.
 
The City Council meeting will begin at 7:00 p.m. at the Council Chambers, Public Safety Complex, 250 5th Avenue North, Edmonds, Washington.  At the time and place stated above, all interested parties will be heard, however, there will be a time restriction for oral comment.  Written comments are welcomed prior to or at the public hearing.
 
If the item is continued to another date because it is not completed, or further information is needed, the date of the continuation will be announced only at the meeting. Additional information may be obtained at the Engineering Department, 121 5th Avenue N., Edmonds.  Telephone:  (425) 771-0220.

Public Invited to Discuss Current Events and Issues with City Council Representatives at Community Town Hall at Swedish Edmonds Hospital

 

(Edmonds, WA) – Edmonds City Councilmembers Adrienne Fraley-Monillas, Neil Tibbott and Dave Teitzel will host a City Council town hall event at Swedish-Edmonds Hospital, 4th Floor Auditorium B, from 6:30 until 8:00 p.m. on March 22, 2017.   This will be an informal opportunity to get to know your Councilmembers and chat with them about important issues facing Edmonds, such as:

 

·       Affordable Housing (incl. senior and low income)

·       Homelessness

·       Managing growth

·       Edmonds Marsh preservation

·       Traffic and pedestrian safety

·       Sunset Avenue

·       Highway 99 revitalization

·       City government inclusiveness

·       Edmonds as a Safe City

·       Downtown parking

·       Any other issues of interest to attendees

 

This will be an excellent opportunity to provide input to your Councilmembers in a casual setting. Edmonds is thriving, but there is more to be done to make this an even greater place to live, work and play and citizen input is important to the local governance process as we work to serve you.   We look forward to visiting with you on March 22!
